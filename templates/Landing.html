features

Our webstite provides a variety of features to help users manage their tasks more effectively.

Task Management

Easily create, edit, and delete tasks to stay organized and manage your to-do list effectively.

Priority Levels

Assign priority levels (Low, Medium, High) to tasks to prioritize your workload and focus on what matters most.

Status Tracking

Track the status of your tasks (To-Do, Doing, Completed) to monitor progress and stay on top of your projects.

User Authentication

Securely sign up, log in, and log out to access your personal task list and keep your data safe.

User-Friendly Interface

Enjoy a clean and intuitive interface that makes task management simple and straightforward.

Due Date Management

Set due dates for tasks to ensure timely completion and avoid missing deadlines.
